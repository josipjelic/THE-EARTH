Research findings

Actionable discoveries for the week ahead

1. Terminator mixing should stay shader-driven

Use a day texture and night texture together, driven by dot(normal, sunDirection), then sharpen the transition with a sigmoid. WebGL Fundamentals shows the exact mix(nightColor, dayColor, mixAmount) pattern.

2. Animated clouds are viable with CanvasTexture

Three.js manual guidance is explicit: redraw the cloud canvas in the render loop and set texture.needsUpdate = true. This is the simplest reliable Genesis-phase animation upgrade.

3. Open-Meteo is browser-friendly

Weather cards can fetch: https://api.open-meteo.com/v1/forecast?latitude=35.68&longitude=139.69&current=temperature_2m,wind_speed_10m,weather_code&timezone=auto and expect a current object with temperature, wind speed, and weather code.

4. Live data feeds are ready for the next phase

ISS tracking is available via https://api.wheretheiss.at/v1/satellites/25544 with no key, while earthquakes can come from https://earthquake.usgs.gov/earthquakes/feed/v1.0/summary/2.5_week.geojson.
